\begin{abstract}
	Nowadays, the Internet has been developed quickly, which the use is not limited only
	to the network management , but also extends to the management of objects ,which
	called the internet of things that is used in several domains and we can mention home
	automation, currently called smart home.
	As part of this research project, our goal is to integrate IoT technology in smart homes
	based on the four basic elements:
	Central organs.
	Sensors.
	Actuators.
	Control interface.
	Keywords: smart home, internet of things, home automation, sensor, actuator, control
	interface.
\end{abstract}
